Inside the pod

Engineered down to the millimeter.

Seven systems work together to make every MTIP Collective Pod feel like a much bigger, much quieter room than its footprint suggests.

LED Lighting

4000K natural light. Dimmable. Anti-glare. Flicker-free. Ra92 color accuracy — what your eyes actually need for a 10-hour day.

Smart Control Panel

Light, fan, and outlets at your fingertips. Universal socket, USB-A, USB-C — works with every plug type on Earth.

Multi-Layer Sound Insulation

Steel + acoustic felt + foam + 12mm PET panel. ISO 23351-1 certified. The wall is doing the work.

Safety Acoustic Glass

6mm shatter-resistant tempered glass. Optional 10mm rear panel for added acoustic depth and a more substantial feel.

Eco Wood Handles

Solid wood with magnetic close. Smart lock or electronic lock optional. Touches you actually want to touch.

Premium Furniture Mounts

Pre-engineered mounting points for desk, bench, lounge, and accessory configurations. Configure it once. Reconfigure forever.

Motion Sensor

Lights and fans auto-on when you enter, auto-off when you leave. Energy-aware by design — and one less thing to think about.
